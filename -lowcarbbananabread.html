Sourdough Chips

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 6-8 mins
Serves: 2-4 People as an entrée, 2 lunchbox snack Serves *Quantity in picture above is not full amount*

For Beginners

1.

Set the oven to around 220 degrees Celsius, on fan forced.

2.

Start by thinly slicing the paninis up so that they are cracker like sizes (0.5 cm or so).

3.

Lay them out on a tray. They can overlap each other but make sure each is somewhat exposed.

4.

Generously cover the slices with oil and salt, then place in the oven.

5.

Keep in the oven for 6-8 minutes or so, and check in making sure they are browning, not burning

Serving:

Taste! Add more salt if it is needed, and serve with a dip of your choice (olive dip goes very well!)
